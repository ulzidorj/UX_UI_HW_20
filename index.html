Sensitummy

App targeted towards users who struggle from sensitive stomach, helps build a routine and track their meals.

Role- UX/UI Design, Research, Testing, Branding and Prototyping

Duration- 3 Weeks

Team of 4

Research

Health History

In order to gain more insight while conducting our research for this app, it was important to understand our participants full health history relating to their stomach. 60% of users had stomach issues 2-3 times a week

Tested Remedies

While there are a lot of remedies out there for people with sensitive stomach, majority do not understand what causes their issues. According to our research 40% of users are un-diagnosed.

Technology as a solution

Out of the 20 participants that were interviewed, 90% said that they would use some sort of an app to help reduce their symptoms.

Problem Statement

"We believe we can help users with stomach issues live more consistently comfortable and pain-free lives by providing an app to make informed nutritional decisions".

Iteration

Based upon our usability testings on our prototype, we made some small changes to keep the experience more simple for our users. These small changes include- indicating which page the user is on in the home navigation, making sure the “x” out buttons are intuitive enough for users and making the log symptoms part simpler.
